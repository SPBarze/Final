What are the Backrooms?

The backrooms are a series of collaborative Urban Legends that are based on the idea of slipping outside of reality by “noclipping” into the supposed Backrooms. The original “backroom” is a infinite loop of office rooms, filled with the rank smell of wet carpet and yellow discolored walls. It is said once one enters the backroom(s), it is almost impossible to leave. Humans themselves cannot comprehend how the backrooms manipulate space because the backrooms themselves exist outside of it.

Different types of Backrooms

Although the backrooms are said to mostly be old rank office buildings, a lot of the time (depending on what system you are looking into) they look very different. Whether it is dark pipeline systems, pools, maybe even arcades or playgrounds, the backrooms can almost be anything because, afterall, they are infinite. No one really knows how many there are (although it is thought they stop at level 9223372036854775807, but this may only be the last level comprehendible by humans.)

Be weary of exits, as majority of the time these exits are traps and bring you back to level 0 or worse levels. You should also sleep at any given opportunity you are safe enough to do so to keep up your sanity.

It is important and highly reccomended to keep a travel journal, as it helps you track your steps and and keep your sanity up. Some levels also have wifi, so refer to the index of levels and entities and be knowledgeable. it could save your life.

It is important to carry a light source with you wherever you go. Some entities are scared of lights and it is always important to see where you are going.

Almond water is a necessity in the backrooms as it is essentially the only source of hydration. It can be used as currency, an ailment, and to repel entities. It can be found in abudance on level 4.

Enjoy your stay - and be careful.
